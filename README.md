# Mobile-sales-Dashboard

 - Developed an interactive Mobile Sales Dashboard in Power BI that transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights. 
 - Tracked KPIs like revenue, sales volume, transactions, and average pricing, and uncovers trends across regions, time, and customer payment behaviour. 
 - Designed it with dynamic filters and drill-down capabilities to enable quick decision-making. 
 - This dashboard can help businesses identify top-performing products, optimize sales strategies, and improve revenue forecasting. 
 - Overall, I focus not just on building dashboards, but on delivering insights that support better business decisions.
